About Us

Welcome to our world of Desinging and stitching

Rozaliya boutique is a charming and trendy boutique nestled in the heart of Edappal. At Rozaliya boutique, we pride ourselves on providing a personalized shopping experience, where our knowledgeable and friendly staff are dedicated to helping customers discover the perfect pieces to enhance their wardrobe.
